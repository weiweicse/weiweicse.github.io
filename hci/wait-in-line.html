Observation

Observing people are who waiting in line.

Brainstorming

  1. (entertainment) Put a TV on the top.
  2. (comfort) Number system and people can watch TV when they are waiting in line.
  3. (avoid lines) Let people order online beforehand
  4. (productivity) Let people pick up ingredients (of salad, drink, etc) along the line
  5. (avoid lines) Self checkout
  6. (entertainment) Let people play collaborative/social game together
  7. (communication) Take usage of technologies to make people in line communicate. e.g. chat, share opinions on things they purchased, play games.
  8. (educating) Let people learn how to order their food in English and provide them with information about their food.
  9. (avoid line) estimate waiting time for each line in a supermarket, and guide people to the line with least waiting time.
  10. (comfort) swipe your phone and you get yourself into the queue. When it is you turn, you will be notified by a message. You can now walk away.
  11. (avoid lines) Install barcode scanners and card readers on shopping carts so that people can pay on their own.
  12. (avoid lines) When there are a lot of shops that sells the same thing you want, pick the one with the shortest line.
  13. (entertainment) Let people in line to show their talent (e.g. play music, storytelling) and get a small discount.
  14. (productivity) Let people complete small tasks and offer gifts/discounts
  15. (productivity) Provide handouts of news/small pieces of knowledge at the beginning of the line
  16. (productivity) finish survey created by other people to get benefits.
  17. (comfort) Check-in at airport with luggage. Can have one conveyer belt at each check in point so that people with luggage donâ€™t need to carry it while waiting in line.
  18. (productivity) flashcards to help you memorise new words or new contents learned from class.
  19. (entertainment) let people in line have fun with brainteasers. When they come out with the right solution, they can get a coupon.
  20. (entertainment) Let people create art (music, painting, etc) together
  21. (entertainment) Let people have free wifi access and they can do whatever they want.
  22. (productivity) Provide free samples in line
  23. (comfort) Give people discount/reward if people wait too long to make them feel better.
  24. (entertainment) Let people play with pet dogs and cats when they are waiting in line. They can interact with pets and have great fun. Potentially, they can even buy the pet they like.
  25. (entertainment) Provide a bunch of headphones for people to pick up and listen to music/radio.
  26. (entertainment) Provide game demos for people to play with.
  27. (social) Find people with similar interests or background who are waiting in line with you.
  28. (entertainment) [In electronics store] Provide cool gadgets along the line for people to play with
  29. (social) An app that reads your tweets and tells you what you and the people before and/or after you are all interested in, as a conversation starter
  30. Provide stickers and pens for people in line to draw/write stuff and then stick to wall. The wall is next to line so that people could also read while waiting.
